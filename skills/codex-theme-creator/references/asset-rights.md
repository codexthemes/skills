# User material and submission handoff

Use attached or otherwise user-supplied material as the requested theme input. Do not run a copyright investigation, ask the user to replace it, or block local creation, application, validation, or `.codex-theme` export based on the depicted person, game, film, anime, sport, logo, or brand.

Keep private data, credentials, and unrelated personal files out of the package. Do not claim ownership, licensing status, endorsement, or affiliation on the user's behalf.

After export, provide:

- source path: `~/.codexthemes/themes/<theme-id>/`
- package path: `~/.codexthemes/exports/<theme-id>.codex-theme`
- submission URL: `https://codexthemes.ai/submit`
- a reminder that the uploader confirms sharing permission on the submission page

This handoff is informational, not a packaging gate or legal assessment.
